TL;DR

Sim-on-wheels is a new safety validation platform for self-driving vehicles that has unique advantages over previous methods as illustrated in Table I of our paper. We want to emphasize that our core contribution is the overall system, and not any individual novel technique(In response to R2’s complaints on unexpected holes on 3d assets, R3’s complaints on insertion rendering as novelty).

Scalibility to Large Amount of Agents (MR#1, R2)

Unlike real-world road testing, Sim-on-Wheels can insert agents itself into the background and can scale to insert many agents without significant loss in rendering speed. We conducted the following experiment under multiple resolution and various number of virtual objects. Our experiment shows that under the same resolution, the rendering speed is not affected much by the increasing number of virtual objects.
